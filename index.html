SPACE DOGFIGHT

Instructions

Player 1 will spawn on the left side of the screen!
Player 2 will spawn on the right side of the screen.
Player 1, use W for up, A for left, S for down, and D for right. Press R to shoot
Player 2, use Up Arrow for Up, Left Arrow for left, S for down, and D for right. Press the spacebar to shoot.
You get 4 chances.
GOOD LUCK!
